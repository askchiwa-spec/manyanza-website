Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get a quote?

WhatsApp quotes are provided within minutes. Email quotes are sent within 2-4 hours during business hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, TigoPesa, bank transfers, and international payment options for overseas clients.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 24-48 hours in advance, though same-day service may be available based on driver availability.

Do you provide insurance coverage?

Yes, all vehicles are covered by comprehensive insurance during transit. Coverage details are provided with your booking confirmation.

Need Emergency Assistance?

If you have an urgent vehicle transit need or require immediate assistance with an ongoing trip, contact our emergency line.
