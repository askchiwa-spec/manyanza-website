Driver Requirements

We maintain high standards to ensure the safety and reliability our customers expect.

Valid Driver's License

Current Tanzanian driver's license with clean driving record

Police Clearance

Recent police clearance certificate (within 6 months)

Professional Experience

Minimum 3 years of professional driving experience

Age Requirement

Between 25-55 years old with good physical and mental health

NIDA Registration

Valid National Identity Card for verification purposes
