About TunaRide

Revolutionizing vehicle transit across Tanzania with trust, reliability, and professional excellence.

Our Mission

To revolutionize Tanzania's vehicle transit industry by providing the most reliable and trusted marketplace that connects vehicle owners with professional, thoroughly vetted drivers. We are committed to ensuring safe, efficient, and transparent transportation solutions that exceed customer expectations while creating sustainable opportunities for our driver partners.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's leading vehicle transit platform, setting new standards for safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction in the transportation industry. We envision a future where vehicle transit is seamless, secure, and accessible to all, powered by technology and driven by professionalism.

Trust & Reliability

What Makes Us Unique

Trusted Driver Marketplace

Every driver on our platform undergoes rigorous vetting including background checks, license verification, police clearance, and professional grading. We ensure only the most qualified drivers handle your vehicle.

Comprehensive Coverage

From local city transfers to long-distance corridor routes across Tanzania, we provide vehicle transit services that connect all major cities and regions, ensuring your vehicle reaches its destination safely.

Reliability & Punctuality

We understand the importance of time in business and personal matters. Our commitment to punctuality and reliable service delivery has made us the preferred choice for thousands of customers.

Professional Excellence

Our team consists of experienced professionals who understand the transportation industry. We provide ongoing training and support to our drivers to maintain the highest standards of service.
